A Bit about Me

Now a little bit about myself. My favorite color is orange. My favorite food is sushi. My favorite activities/hobbies are going to the beach (I live on the beach). Spending time with my friends and family. Football is my biggest passion I played my entire life. I now currently work for the UGA football team. My favorite type of music is rap and country. My favorite rappers are Kodak Black, Future, and NBA Youngboy. My favorite country singers are Jason Aldean and Florida Georgia Line.

I've really liked future ever since he came out with his debut album called Pluto that was released on April 13th, 2012. His style has been an inspiration for rappers around the world with his distinct raspy type voice. He has been a spotlight especially for the record label called A1 Recordings, Freebandz, and Epic records. I hope that he continues to make music. His last album was one of my favorites of all time.

I thought originally that I wanted to move to NYC following my mom, but after seeing everyone live in the snow for almost 8 months of the year. I have decided that it really isn't in my taste. Delray is more my style.
